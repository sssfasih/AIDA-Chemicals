About

Find Out More About Us

AIDA is a leading chemicals company of Pakistan, dealing in a complete range of textile and specialty chemicals.

The company was formed with a view to offer competitive, top of the line products to customers and provides innovative solutions to their needs. We pride ourselves in creating value for our customers, through continuous product improvement and developments, efficient after sales and technical services and innovative solutions for their day to day problems. Today the services and operations of aida are distributed within the country and soon we reached across the borders of pakistan. We are delivering an array of solutions to customers belonging to various segments of textile processing and related industries.

OUR VISION

AIDA has leaded the way in major innovations and developments relating to the textile industry. We will continue to do so we’ll into the future. We serve, with maximum of our capability, to ensure that our customers have the most up-to-date products and we strive to ensure that every state-of-the-art product or technique is available to them. We are constantly breaking new grounds in the textile industry. We devote countless resources to make sure that we offer the best and most innovative products.

OUR MISSION

To provide our customers with high quality alternative chemical products, through continuous product development, improvement and technology we will strive to offer competitive top of the line products and technical services, thus creating value for our customers by addressing their needs. To expand geographically into regional markets and develop a network of marketing and trading activities to facilitate growth of our business.
